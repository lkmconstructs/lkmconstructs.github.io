The Solution

Three layers of AI infrastructure

Aondor — Governance Layer. Runtime governance for live AI workflows. Dual-pipeline: PatternWall (pre-inference) + Sensus (post-inference). 0% native safety on frontier models becomes 98.3% detection with Aondor deployed. Model-agnostic, deterministic, auditable. Patent pending.

Parallax — Context Layer. Cognitive Operations Layer reduces user steering burden across AI interactions. 16-module inference pipeline. Benchmarked across 8 models with near-frontier lift on mid-tier models. Patent pending.

Voiceprint — Authorship Layer. Preserves recognizable writing voice under AI assistance. Captures and maintains your voice fingerprint across AI-assisted content. Runs locally. Currently in beta.
