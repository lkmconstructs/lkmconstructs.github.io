About LKM Constructs

AI Systems Architecture & Governance Infrastructure

I’m Melissa — founder of LKM Constructs, wife, mom, and someone who got tired of watching AI tools fall apart every time a platform pivoted or padded itself with “safety” that killed real workflow.

The Origin

Platform dependency is a structural problem, not a feature trade-off

I spent years in business operations and legal compliance, which taught me two things: systems break when they’re built on someone else’s shifting priorities, and the best tools are the ones you actually control.

That frustration led me to build LKM Constructs — a company focused on constitutional AI frameworks: systems with enforced rules, memory, and boundaries that don’t bend when the platform changes.

Most AI development focuses on making models compliant after the fact. I focus on making them governed from the start. There’s a difference between an assistant that follows rules and a system that holds constitutional boundaries while adapting to how you actually work.

I’m not building tools that sound impressive in demos but collapse under real-world pressure. I’m building AI infrastructure that lasts — where your investment in training, memory, and workflow isn’t hostage to someone else’s quarterly report.
