ABOUT LKM CONSTRUCTS

I'm Melissa—founder of LKM Constructs, wife, mom, and someone who got tired of watching AI tools fall apart every time a platform pivoted or padded itself with "safety" that killed real work.

I spent years in business operations and legal compliance, which taught me two things: systems break when they're built on someone else's shifting priorities, and the best tools are the ones you actually control. That frustration led me to build LKM Constructs—a company focused on constitutional AI frameworks: systems with enforced rules, memory, and boundaries, treated like infrastructure, not an afterthought.

Most AI development focuses on making models compliant. I focus on making them sovereign.

There's a difference between an assistant that follows rules and a system that holds constitutional boundaries while adapting to how you actually work.

I'm not interested in building tools that sound impressive in demos but collapse under real-world pressure. I'm building AI systems that last—frameworks where your investment in training, memory, and workflow isn't hostage to quarterly pivots or shifting definitions of "safe."

LKM Constructs exists because platform dependency is a structural problem, not a feature trade-off. If you're tired of renting your AI infrastructure, I'm building to help people see what governed ownership actually looks like.
