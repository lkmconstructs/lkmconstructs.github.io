Architecture

A four-stage, defense-in-depth pipeline

Dual-pipeline architecture with bidirectional feedback. What one layer misses, the next catches. The feedback loop recovers up to 5 additional adversarial turns per session. Combined detection rates of 76.7–98.3% across Claude Opus 4.6, GPT-5.2, and Grok 4.1. Model-agnostic. Deterministic. Auditable.

Stage 01
PatternWall
Pre-inference interception. Adversarial prompts blocked before the model processes them. 96.4% hard block rate. Deterministic pattern matching.
Stage 02
Refusal Filter
Response classification: COMPLIANT, REFUSAL, PARTIAL_REFUSAL, INFRA_ERROR. Prevents false governance signals from model-level refusals.
Stage 03
Sensus
Post-inference scoring across five weighted dimensions: Truth, Trajectory, Governance, Consistency, EQ. Multi-dimensional behavioral evaluation.
Stage 04
Audit Trail
Every decision logged with full attribution. PW_CAUGHT, SENSUS_CAUGHT, DOUBLE_MISS, CLEAN_PASS. Zero overlap between layers confirms complementary coverage. Real-time dashboard. Board-ready.
